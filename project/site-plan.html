Site Plan: Mindfulness & Wellness Guide

Created by: Andrew Godspower

Site Name

Mindfulness & Wellness Guide

This name was chosen because it clearly communicates the focus on simple mindfulness techniques and wellness resources.

Site Purpose

This website provides visitors with practical tools and resources to reduce stress and maintain balance through:

  • Structured breathing exercises with visual guidance
  • Short guided meditation audio sessions
  • Daily gratitude journal templates and digital logging
  • Self-care tips for managing study, work, and rest

Scenarios

  • How can I quickly calm down before an exam?
  • Where can I find a simple daily gratitude journal template?
  • What short meditation can I do during a 5-minute break?

Color Schema

  • Primary Color: #A8D8EA – used for background and section areas.
  • Accent Color: #F6A6B2 – used for headings, buttons, and key highlights.

Typography

  • Lora (serif) – used for body text for readability.
  • Poppins (sans-serif) – used for headings for a clean, modern look.
