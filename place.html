About Yankari National Park

  • Country: Nigeria
  • Capital: Abuja
  • State: Bauchi
  • Population (Bauchi State): ~6.5 million
  • Area (Yankari Park): 2,244 km²
  • Official Language: English
  • Major Languages: Hausa, Fulfulde
  • Currency: Nigerian Naira (₦)
  • Time Zone: West Africa Time (UTC+1)

Overview

Yankari National Park is Nigeria’s premier wildlife reserve, home to elephants, lions, hippos, and warm spring-fed Wikki Warm Springs.

Wildlife

  • African Elephants
  • West African Lions
  • Hippopotamus
  • Baboon Troops
